Making Grilled Cheese Sandwichs

Ingredients:

2 slices of Bread (readers choice on the type)

1/4 of grated Cheese or 2 slices of cheese (readers choice on the type)

1 tablespoon of Butter (or how ever much you enjoy, I'm not here to judge)

Cooking instrument (Stove & Pan, Toaster & Microwave, or Oven & cookie sheet)

This is how you make a grilled cheese sandwich with a Stove.

Step One:
Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat, then place and rub one slice of bread into the buttered pan.
Step Two:
Add the Cheese on top of the bread and cover the pan until the cheese almost completely melts.
Step Three:
When the cheese is almost completely melted top the Grilled Cheese off with the 2nd slice of bread making sure to apply a small amount of pressure to get the cheese to stick and flip the sandwich until both sides are evenly toasted. Now serve it and enjoy!

This is how you make a grilled cheese sandwich with a Toaster and Microwave.

Step One:
Place the 2 slices of bread into the toast and set the toaster to the perfect setting of goldening the two slices, (optional) around the same time place the butter into the microwave and heat it up for around 5 to 10 seconds so that it is soft when spreading it onto the slices.
Step Two:
Once the bread slices are ready spread butter onto the one side of the each slice and place onto a mcrowave safe plate, then add the cheese, top the sandwich and place into the microwave for 10 to 20 seconds or until the cheese is melted.
Step Three:
Once it is finished take it out of the microwave and enjoy your Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

This is how you make a grilled cheese sandwich with a Oven.

Step One:
Preheat the Oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit, and butter both slices of bread before placing one slice it onto a cookie sheet butter side down.
Step Two:
Place the cheese on top of the slice of bread that is on the cookie sheet and top the sandwich off with the 2nd slice of bread with the butter side facing up.
Step Three:
Baked the sandwich of about 5 minutes before flippling it and baking for an additional 3 to 4 minutes. Once done serve it and Enjoy!
